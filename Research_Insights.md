# Research Insights on Edge AI

## General Introduction
Edge AI refers to the deployment of artificial intelligence algorithms on devices at the edge of the network, such as smartphones, IoT devices, and other decentralized hardware, instead of relying on centralized data centers or cloud computing resources. This approach enables real-time data processing, reduces latency, conserves bandwidth, enhances privacy, and provides more reliable performance in environments with intermittent connectivity. Edge AI is critical for applications requiring immediate decision-making, such as autonomous vehicles, smart home devices, and industrial automation.
